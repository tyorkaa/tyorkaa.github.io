My name is Valentina Terkova. Or just Tyorka. I live and work in Komsomolsk-on-Amur. This town is located in the very east of Russia, - you will not find it on the map without a hint. I work, by the way, as a radio presenter. Every day I wake up at 5-6am and present my voice on the air to cheer peop le up. Or vice versa. Basically this is what makes me smile even early in the morning.

I do felting as well. I found wool is the material that allows you to bring any idea to life. The one that endures mistakes. The one that gives warmth.

Ceramics is my second love. Love and the teacher. This is what teaches me zen. If you wonder why, just imagine how all your many day-long work come to nothing, perishing in the oven. All that remains meanwhile is to smile, and to accept this experience. And to continue.

So this is the way I live. I smile, give warmth and continue to work, despite any of the mistakes.
